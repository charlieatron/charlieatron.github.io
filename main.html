About Hand Differences

Hand differences are a birth defect that change the shape of someone's hand. It could be a lack of a few fingers, the fusing of two or more fingers, among others. Most of the time, people are born with this condition and live with it their entire lives. The problem is that hand differences don't have a place in society. Unlike other disabilities, this one is known only to those who have the condition. Because of this, when a "normal" person meets someone with a hand difference, the "normal" person is taken by surprise, because they didn't know this kind of thing was possible.
