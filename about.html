About this Site

This site was made as part of a Social Justice project for my tenth grade English class. It was made to bring awareness to congenital hand deformities, and try to make them a more accepted aspect of society. Through the creation and enaction of this website, my hope is that more people will acknowledge and understand hand differences, something that isn't incredibly uncommon.
